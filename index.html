How to Play

Stats:
Health, Attack
Defense, Speed

On your turn:

Play a card(Place base creature, evolution, or land)
Evolutions can only be placed on their previous form
Lands are persistent effects, each player can have 1 active land at a time
Enter Combat

Combat:

Creature with higher SPEED does damage first.
Same SPEED means damage is done simultaneously.
Subtract damage from HEALTH.
Damage = (ATTACK - DEFENSE) * (2 for strengths or 1/2 for weaknesses)
